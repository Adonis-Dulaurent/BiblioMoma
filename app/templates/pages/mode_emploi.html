How to use BiblioMoMA

BiblioMoMA makes your research easier by providing you with a bibliography on each artwork or artist within the museum's collections. It is easy to use:

  1. Go to the Search page
  2. Enter the artwork or artist you are interested in
  3. A notice will be displayed, detailed important information (such as date of production or dates of birth and death, origin, artistic movement...). Below, you can generate a bibliography sourced from OpenAlex.
  4. Once you have generated the bibliography, you may add items to your basket. Remember to sign up or log in before you do so!
