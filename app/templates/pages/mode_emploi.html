How to use BiblioMoMA

BiblioMoMA makes your research easier by providing you with a bibliography on each artwork or artist within the museum's collections. It is easy to use: in the search bar, enter the artwork or artist you are interested in. A notice will be displayed, detailed important information (such as date of production or dates of birth and death, origin, artistic movement...) as well as a bibliography sourced from OpenAlex. The Advanced search page allows you to specify your request.
